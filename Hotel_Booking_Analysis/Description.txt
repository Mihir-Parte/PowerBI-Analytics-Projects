- Dataset Overview: Contains 119,390 booking records covering guest details, stay information, and cancellations.

- Key Fields: LeadTime, BookingDate, StayDuration, IsCanceled, RoomType.

- Data Sources: Hotel management system exports.

- Data Cleaning: Filled missing values in RoomType, converted BookingDate to datetime format, and standardized text data.

- Key Visuals: Cancellation heatmap, average stay duration chart, and seasonal trend insights.
